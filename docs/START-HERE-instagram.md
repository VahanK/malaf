## 1. Create the account — the no-ban way
New accounts get watched hardest in week one. Follow this exactly:

1. **New email** for the brand (fresh Gmail or `hello@work-withme.com`) — not tied to your personal logins.
2. Sign up on your **phone, on normal wifi or mobile data**. **No VPN/proxy** on a brand-new account — that's the #1 trigger.
3. **Handle:** `workwith.lb` (first choice) or `workwithme.lb` / `getworkwith`. The `.lb` reads local, like the competitor's `linki.lb`. Grab the same name on TikTok even if unused.
4. **Fully fill the profile BEFORE the first post** (pic, bio, link). Empty accounts that suddenly post a lot look like bots.
5. Switch to a **Business/Creator account**: Settings → Account type → Creator. Category: **Product/Service** or **Software**.
6. **Warm up 2–3 days:** follow ~10–20 relevant accounts/day, like and comment like a human, *before* you start posting hard.

**Never:** follow/unfollow bots, engagement pods, bought followers, or scheduling tools that log in for you. Post images by hand. That's what keeps you safe.

## 4. What's actually tappable on Instagram (so the CTA works)
Instagram only lets you put a **real clickable link** in three places. The posts
are already written to match each one:

| Surface | Clickable? | The CTA to use (baked into the art) |
|---------|-----------|--------------------------------------|
| **Bio link** | ✅ one link | `work-withme.com` — everything points here |
| **Story** | ✅ **link sticker** | the story CTA says *"Tap the link"* → add a link sticker to `work-withme.com` on every story |
| **Feed post** | ❌ no links in captions | the post CTA says *"Link in bio 👆"* — don't waste it on a raw URL nobody can tap |
| **DMs** | ✅ you can send links | reply to every "how?" with your link |
| **Comments** | ❌ links aren't tappable | use them to say "link in bio" / answer questions |

So: **stories get link stickers, feed posts send people to the bio, DMs close
them.** Never paste a bare URL in a feed caption expecting taps — it won't work.

## 5. Posting plan
**Feed order** (1/day; the grid is your first impression):
`brand` → `hook` → `what-it-is` → `found-booked` → `how-it-works` →
`vs-linktree` → `qr` → `founder` → `arabic` → `cta`.
**Pin** `hook`, `what-it-is`, `founder` to the top of the profile.

**Per-trade content** — each trade has BOTH a **feed post** (`cat-*-post`) and a
**story** (`cat-*`): photographer · makeup · tutor · designer · videographer ·
trainer · nail tech · social media manager.
- Drop **one trade as a feed post ~2×/week** (rotate trades) — evergreen, keeps the grid fresh.
- Roll the **story version any day**, with a **link sticker** + a poll ("Sound familiar? 😅"), and **save it to the "For you" highlight** so it doesn't vanish in 24h.
- Whoever replies "that's literally me" → that's a founder lead. DM them your link.

## 6. Do / don't
- ✅ Post by hand, slowly, fully-set-up profile first.
- ✅ Stories get link stickers; feed sends to bio; DMs close.
- ✅ Watch profile visits → link taps → signups (not follower count).
- ❌ No bots, pods, bought followers, auto-DM, or login-based schedulers.
- ❌ Don't dump all posts on day one.
- ❌ Don't run ads yet — post organically first, then boost only what already worked (video-ad playbook: `docs/ad-templates.md`).
