# WorkWith — Instagram launch & account guide

Everything to stand up the brand's Instagram (a **fresh account, not your
personal one**) and post the studio content. Nothing here touches WhatsApp
automation or any Meta API — you post by hand, like any normal account, so
there's nothing to get banned for.

Grab the images from **`/studio/posts`** (Download PNG). Feed posts are
1080×1080; the per-trade posts are 1080×1920 stories.

---

## 1. Create the account (15 min)
- [ ] New email for the brand (e.g. `hello@work-withme.com` or a fresh Gmail) — **not** tied to your personal logins.
- [ ] Sign up for Instagram with that email. Use mobile data or normal wifi; don't use a VPN/proxy on a brand-new account (looks bot-like).
- [ ] **Handle:** `workwith.lb` or `workwithme.lb` (the `.lb` reads local, like the competitor's `linki.lb`). Grab the matching one on TikTok too even if you don't use it yet.
- [ ] Switch to a **Business/Creator account** (Settings → Account type). Creator is fine. This unlocks the link, contact button, and insights.
- [ ] Category: **Software / Product-Service**.

### Don't get flagged (your ban worry)
- New accounts are watched hardest in week one. **Post slowly** — see the cadence below, don't dump 12 posts on day one.
- **No automation, no follow/unfollow tools, no bought followers.** That's what gets accounts killed — not posting images.
- Follow ~10–20 relevant accounts a day, max. Engage like a human.
- Fill the profile out *fully* before your first post — empty profiles that suddenly post a lot look fake.

## 2. Profile setup
- [ ] **Profile photo:** export the `brand` post (the WorkWith. tile) → crop square. Keep the lime dot visible.
- [ ] **Name field** (the bold searchable line, not the handle): `WorkWith · Freelancer pages` — put a keyword here, it's searchable.
- [ ] **Bio** (English-first, one Arabic line):
  ```
  A professional page for Lebanese freelancers ⚡
  Your work, your prices, your link — free.
  صفحتك المهنية بثواني.
  👇 ابدأ من هون
  ```
- [ ] **Link:** `https://work-withme.com` (or a specific signup page). When you have a Linktree-style need later, WorkWith is literally that — but for now one link is cleaner.
- [ ] **Action buttons:** add the WhatsApp/Email contact button (Edit profile → Contact options) so DMs aren't the only channel.

## 4. Feed launch order
Your grid is the first impression. Post in this order, **1 per day** for the
first ~10 days (or 3 on day one *only if* the profile is fully set up, then
slow to daily):

1. `brand` — the cover
2. `hook` — "Your work is studio-level. Is your link?"
3. `what-it-is` — the page + what's on it
4. `found-booked` — Instagram finds you, your page books you
5. `how-it-works` — the four steps
6. `vs-linktree` — not just a link
7. `qr` — one scan → your page
8. `founder` — first 50 get featured
9. `arabic` — the Arabic-audience post
10. `cta` — your page is waiting

**Pin** `hook`, `what-it-is`, and `founder` to the top of your profile once
they're up (tap post → Pin to profile).

---

## 5. Stories — the per-trade series
The 8 `cat-*` posts are built at story size (1080×1920), text kept in the
middle band so IG's top bar and reply box never cover it.

- [ ] Roll **one trade per day** as a story (photographer, makeup, tutor, …). Each freelancer sees *their own* post — that's the point.
- [ ] Add a **link sticker** to `work-withme.com` on each.
- [ ] Add a **poll or question sticker** ("Sound familiar? 😅") — replies boost reach and start DMs.
- [ ] **Save each to the "For you" highlight** so they don't vanish in 24h.
- [ ] Screenshot anyone who replies "that's so me" — those are your first founder leads.

---

## 8. What NOT to do
- ❌ No follow/unfollow bots, engagement pods, or bought followers.
- ❌ Don't post all 18 assets in one day.
- ❌ Don't run ads yet — post organically first, then only boost a post that already did well (the ad playbook lives in `docs/ad-templates.md`).
- ❌ Don't put your personal number as the only contact — use the brand WhatsApp/email.
