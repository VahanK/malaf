# Parking Lot — ideas wait here, evaluated at monthly triage or gate resolutions only

**Rule:** one line per idea. Nothing here gets built until ≥5 paying users ask, a gate resolves, or monthly triage promotes it. (CLAUDE.md red lines still apply.)

- **Meetups + lanyards** (vahan, Jul 19): Malaf freelancer meetups where attendees get a physical lanyard/badge with their QR card — wearable malaf for expos, wedding fairs, campus events. Fits the identity-object thesis + community layer. Slot: September+, after campus playbook ignites; pairs with the founder printed cards. Cost ~$2–4/lanyard.
- **Custom domains** (Jul 19): paid add-on, v1.1 — cheap on Vercel, DNS support burden, low segment demand. Trigger: ≥5 paying requests.
- **PDF upload for case cards** (writers segment): v1.1 — excerpt cards ship in v1.
- **Deposit requests ("50% to book")**: v1.1 candidate — likely early cluster, watch for it in founder conversations.
- **Gulf/diaspora expansion**: opens at Lebanon farm slowdown or $1k MRR — kit already written (docs/validation-kit-gulf-shelf.md).
- **Phase 2 directory/marketplace (Khedme resurrection)**: locked behind $2k MRR or month 12.
- **Supplier/shipping/fulfillment directory** (Jul 20, demand data): ~560 requests in Wolf of Bey channel — company directory, not freelancer front-office; marketplace-adjacent.
- **Cross-referrals between Malaf freelancers** (Jul 20, demand data): buyers ask for bundles (logo+site+ads); marketplace-adjacent, post-validation only.
- **Client-initiated proposal/estimate request** (vahan, Jul 20): a client on the public page requests a scoped proposal/estimate from the freelancer (not just a generic quote-request CTA), freelancer responds with an estimate the client can review. Needs scoping vs the existing quote flow — could be a variant of quote_requests, not necessarily a marketplace feature. Watch for it explicitly in founder conversations before building.
- **Replacement viral/growth loop** (vahan, Jul 20): RESOLVED into a plan, not yet built — see `docs/launch-plan.md` §3 (Phase 3). Recommendation there: ship a document-footer mention (quotes/invoices/receipts only, never the public card) now; hold the referral-credit mechanic until ≥5 paying freelancers ask for it; let the campus playbook carry word-of-mouth in September.
- **POSITIONING SHIFT — "portfolio/website builder first" (vahan, Jul 22):** after reviewing reference sites (Adobe Portfolio, Tally, Cinderella's Studio, Barmajino, Hjezle, r/lebanon freelance thread), vahan chose the **portfolio/website builder** as the product WorkWith leads with — the thing an ad promises and a freelancer signs up for. This reframes, not discards, what's built: the 3 public-page layouts + presets + templates ARE now the core product; payments/quotes/invoices/the chaser become **paid upgrades a free page graduates into**, not the headline. The references pointed at 3–4 possible products (website builder / booking à la Hjezle / marketplace / agency-ops); vahan picked builder-first and the rest are gated:
  - **Booking system (Hjezle-style calendar + availability + intake)** — parked; a real scheduling engine doesn't exist yet, revisit as a paid add-on once the builder has real users.
  - **Freelance marketplace** — already parked behind $2k MRR / month 12 (see Phase-2 line above); builder-first is a *better* on-ramp to it (installed base first, then connect the two sides).
  - **Agency-ops / "what an agency can do low-friction"** — parked; watch whether early users are solo freelancers or small agencies before scoping.
  - **NEXT BUILD this implies (not yet built):** a real in-UI **page editor** — add/reorder/edit portfolio blocks, upload images, pick template, edit services — because today every page (Layla, Nour, demos) was populated by SQL, not by the freelancer. A website builder with no editor isn't the product. This is the concrete work builder-first unlocks.
- **"Expand beyond freelancers / AI-generates-the-profile / multi-market AR+FR across 8 countries" pitch** (Jul 22, external advice relayed by vahan): three separable claims, decided independently —
  1. **More presets (consultant, coach, architect, lawyer, event planner) — ACCEPTED, building now.** Free under the existing architecture (CLAUDE.md §0: new preset = JSON config, zero new code). Not a pivot, just filling out the preset list the system was always designed to hold.
  2. **AI-ASSISTS onboarding (draft bio/services from a few answers, freelancer edits before publishing) — ACCEPTED IN PRINCIPLE, deferred, build later.** vahan confirmed "ai onboarded assisting later" (Jul 22). Must stay assistive-and-editable, never autonomous-and-final — CLAUDE.md's "ChatGPT test" bans generated text AS the selling point (trivially copyable, no moat), but AI as a faster path INTO the existing editable onboarding flow doesn't violate that. Revisit once the core template/preset work settles.
  3. **8-country multi-market expansion (Jordan/Egypt/UAE/Saudi/Morocco/Tunisia/Pakistan/India/Nigeria) — DECLINED for now, not deferred-yes.** Lebanon-first isn't arbitrary (CLAUDE.md: real personal access, WhatsApp-native local rails like Whish/OMT that don't exist the same way elsewhere). Going wide before Lebanon has paying customers multiplies support/payment-rail/translation burden for markets with zero founder access advantage — exactly the scope-creep CLAUDE.md's gates exist to block. French string support is cheap (i18n scaffolding already exists for AR/EN) and can be added anytime without implying market expansion.
