What this means for Malaf

  • Demand is real and recurring. 3,812 asks; 426 people asked three or more times. A freelancer's card gets repeat chances to be re-shared.
  • The slot is verifiably unoccupied. In 2,424 self-promo posts over four years: 768 Instagram handles, 598 phone numbers, 41 website links, zero link-in-bio tools. The gap between "Instagram profile" and "real website" has no incumbent in this market.
  • The community prescribes Malaf's blocks to itself: portfolio, testimonials, before/after, visible prices are its own advice for looking credible.
  • Local rails win: Whish/OMT + USDT mentions outnumber PayPal + Payoneer ~4:1 among sellers.
  • Founder-15 target order: web/Shopify devs → video editors & UGC creators → designers → photographers → media buyers/marketers.
